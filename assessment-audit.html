Scalability / Performance / Manageability / Security / High Availability Assessment

Most of your legacy application does not scale because their traffic/processing need has grown up significantly, however, their technology stack is still very old; they lack modern technologies like load balancing, distributed clustering, NoSQL, in-memory database, and distributed caching. We have been applying those modern technologies to solve our customers legacy application issues for a long time. We can assess your existing application and recommend the improvements and implement them on a milestone basis.
We can help you in all possible ways from performance analysis to improvement -- a complete transition of your existing applications towards meeting more data and processing demands.

Our Difference

When you ask us about the system performance, we use the effective monitoring tool to find out in and out of the box. We carefully perform the benchmarking right from your application stack and all the way to the network and storage stack. You do not need a costly product suite to buy and invest a huge amount for its implementation. Our approach is rather simple -- giving you many scripts so that you know the system information quickly and efficiently. The result is real data and a series of specific recommendations. With this information, you decide what makes sense for your organization. We work as part of your IT team to design, implement, and manage a system performance monitoring program covering your applications, servers, LAN, databases, WAN, and VLAN. Together, we will do more than assuring seamless performance -- we will build a system that will quickly and easily serve your organization.
