Cloud Concierge

In the last few years, cloud computing has grown from being a promising business concept to one of the fastest growing segments of the IT industry.
Now, recession-hit companies are increasingly realizing that simply by tapping into the cloud they can gain fast access to best-of-breed business applications or drastically boost their infrastructure resources, all at negligible cost.
One of the main problems is that IT Management doesn't want to give up control of sensitive data to a nebulous cloud-based computing architecture. Leading IT experts feel there needs to be on-demand scalability, fully automated and audit friendly.
Synectiks is a company that can provide fully scalable and secured Cloud Solutions or be the Cloud Partner to give your firm the Power of 2. As an extension of your internal IT resources, Synectiks can provide:

  • SLA Monitoring
  • Network Segmentation Audits
  • Automation & Orchestration
  • Security Testing
  • Cloud Back-Up/Storage
  • On-Demand Resources
  • Disaster Recovery Roadmap
